Wonders of the World

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials, built along the northern borders of China to protect against invasions.

Petra, Jordan

Petra is an ancient city carved into the rose-red cliffs of southern Jordan. It is famous for its archaeological and historical significance.

Christ the Redeemer, Brazil

This iconic statue of Jesus Christ stands atop the Corcovado mountain in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and overlooks the city.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is a well-preserved Inca citadel located in the Andes Mountains of Peru. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and an archaeological marvel.

Other Wonders of the World

Here are some other wonders of the world.

Chichen Itza, Mexico

Chichen Itza is a complex of Mayan ruins on the Yucatan Peninsula, known for its step pyramid, temples, and ancient astronomical observatory.

  • Details

Roman Colosseum, Italy

The Colosseum, also known as the Flavian Amphitheatre, is an ancient amphitheater in Rome, Italy, known for its gladiatorial contests and events.

  • Details

Taj Mahal, India

The Taj Mahal is an exquisite white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

  • Details

Angkor Wat, Cambodia

Angkor Wat is a massive temple complex and the largest religious monument in the world, located in Siem Reap, Cambodia. It is a stunning example of Khmer architecture and artistry.

  • Details

The Pyramids of Giza, Egypt

The Pyramids of Giza, including the Great Pyramid, are iconic symbols of ancient Egypt. They have fascinated people for centuries with their architectural precision and historical significance.

  • Details

The Amazon Rainforest, South America

The Amazon Rainforest is the world's largest tropical rainforest and is often referred to as the "lungs of the Earth" due to its critical role in oxygen production and carbon dioxide absorption.
